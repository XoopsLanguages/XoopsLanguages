Developer: WF Team

Introduction
 WF-Links creates a section for categorising and displaying links to other websites
 
 Purpose
 When you want to provide links to other sites on the Internet, you will need a way to categorise and display these links in an orderly fashion with descriptions about each of them. WF-Links does just that.
 
 Key Features
 
- Categories and subcategories
- User submissions
- Permissions on categories
